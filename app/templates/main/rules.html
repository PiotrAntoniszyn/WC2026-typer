How It Works
  • Prediction window — Submit or update your score prediction for any match up until it locks.
  • Lock time — Predictions close automatically 30 minutes before kick-off. After that, no changes are possible.
  • Knockout scoring — Points are awarded for the 90-minute result. Extra time and penalties do not change the outcome used for scoring.
  • Champion pick — Pick your tournament winner before the window closes. Correct pick earns a 20-point bonus on top of all match points.
  • Result corrections — If an official result is corrected, your points are recalculated automatically.
